About Breathe & Be

A safe space designed by students, for students, to help navigate the pressures of school and life.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide simple, accessible, and effective tools to support student mental wellbeing. We believe that everyone deserves a moment to "breathe and be"‚Äîto pause, reflect, and find the strength within themselves. This app is a judgment-free zone where you can manage stress, tackle procrastination, and build a more positive mindset, one small step at a time.

Our Story

Breathe & Be was created as a major project by a dedicated student who understands the challenges of modern academic life. Seeing the need for a centralized, supportive resource, I combined my skills in design and development to build an app that I would want to use myself. Every feature, from the Vent Machine to the Procrastination Planner, was built with the student experience in mind.
