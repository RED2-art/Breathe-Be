Reach Out for Support

If you need someone to talk to, here are trusted people and services ready to listen and help.

School Contacts

Year Advisor Email

Your first point of contact for academic or personal support related to your year group.

Deputy Principal Email

Contact for more serious concerns or if you need to escalate an issue within the school.

School Counselor Email

Professional help for emotional wellbeing, mental health, or personal problems.

External Support Services

Kids Helpline

Free, confidential 24/7 phone and online counseling service for young people aged 5-25. Call 1800 55 1800.

Beyond Blue

Support and information for anxiety, depression, and suicide prevention. Call 1300 22 4636.

Headspace

Mental health support specifically for 12-25 year olds with online and in-person services.

Lifeline Australia

24/7 crisis support and suicide prevention. Call 13 11 14 for immediate help.

eheadspace

Free online and telephone mental health support for young people and their families.

SANE Australia

Provides support, research, and advocacy for people affected by complex mental health issues.
